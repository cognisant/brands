# Bath ASU

## Name

Correct:

- Bath ASU

The name is the word Bath, followed by an uppercase abbreviation ASU (which stands for Aseptic Services Unit) separated by a space.

Incorrect:

- BathASU
- Bath Asu
- bath ASU
- Bath A S U
- Bath Aseptic Services
